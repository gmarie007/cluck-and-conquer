🐣 How to Play

Cluck & Conquer is a gamified productivity app. Assign your real-world tasks to eggs, tend them as they mature, and hatch a surprise chicken when the work is done. Complete tasks, grow your flock.

The Egg Bank

Each cycle gives you 5 eggs to work with. Your first bank is all Basic eggs. After that, banks are a mix of Basic, Fancy, and Rare — rarer eggs hatch rarer chickens.

Placing an Egg

Click any empty nest to place an egg. You'll be asked to name the task that egg represents. One egg per nest — choose wisely!

Egg Phases

Eggs mature over 20 minutes across 4 phases (5 minutes each). Watch for the glow to grow. When the egg shows 🐣 it's ready to hatch. An egg left untouched for 12 hours goes rotten and must be cleared without reward.

Hatching

Click a mature egg and confirm your task is complete to hatch it. A chicken will appear in the barnyard — the tier of your egg influences which chicken you receive.

Egg Tiers & Chicken Odds

Basic50% Basic chicken · 50% Fancy chicken
Fancy50% Fancy chicken · 50% Rare chicken
Rare25% Basic · 50% Fancy · 25% Rare chicken

Completing a Cycle

Resolve all 5 eggs — by hatching or deleting — to complete a cycle and claim a fresh egg bank with mixed tiers.

The Catalog

There are 15 collectible chickens across three tiers. Open the Catalog to see which you've discovered and how many times each has been hatched.
